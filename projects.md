## - Social Media & Perception of Wealth  
**Visual Wealth Cue Detection in Instagram Posts**  
*Python • Florence-2 • OCR • GPT-4o • Computer Vision • Multimodal Analysis*

As part of a broader research project on social signaling and digital inequality, I developed a multimodal pipeline to analyze visual wealth cues in Instagram posts associated with Forbes-affiliated lists (e.g., Billionaires, Top Creators, 30 Under 30).

The system integrates AI-powered image captioning, OCR, and lexicon-based matching to detect luxury-related objects, settings, and activities at scale.

### 🔧 Tools & Methods Used:
- `Florence-2-large` (Microsoft) for image captioning & vision-language processing  
- `GPT-4o-mini` (OpenAI) for wealth cue lexicon generation  
- `Tesseract OCR` with preprocessing for robust in-image text extraction  
- Custom tone/context taggers for hypothesis testing

This work supports scalable, automated analysis of visual signaling in elite digital spaces and enables empirical testing of hypotheses related to conspicuous consumption and audience reactions.
